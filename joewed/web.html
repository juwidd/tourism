Welcome to Mabini, Batangas

Discover pristine beaches, world-class diving spots, and breathtaking sunsets in the Philippines' hidden gem

Top Attractions

🏖️

Anilao Beach

Famous for its crystal-clear waters and vibrant marine life. Perfect for swimming, snorkeling, and beach relaxation.

🤿

Diving Capital

World-renowned diving destination with over 40 dive sites featuring colorful coral reefs and diverse marine species.

🌅

Sombrero Island

A picturesque island perfect for day trips, offering stunning views and excellent snorkeling opportunities.

🏛️

Galunggong Festival

Annual celebration honoring the town's fishing heritage with colorful parades and local delicacies.

Things to Do

🏊‍♂️

Swimming & Beach Activities

Enjoy pristine beaches with calm, clear waters perfect for swimming and water sports.

🐠

Scuba Diving & Snorkeling

Explore vibrant coral reefs and encounter diverse marine life in world-class diving spots.

🛥️

Island Hopping

Visit nearby islands and discover hidden coves, secluded beaches, and stunning viewpoints.

🍽️

Local Cuisine

Savor fresh seafood and traditional Filipino dishes at beachfront restaurants and local eateries.

Plan Your Visit

Getting Here

📍 Location: Mabini, Batangas, Philippines

🚗 By Car: 2-3 hours from Manila via SLEX and Batangas highways

🚌 By Bus: Take a bus to Batangas City, then jeepney to Mabini

🌡️ Best Time: November to May (dry season)
